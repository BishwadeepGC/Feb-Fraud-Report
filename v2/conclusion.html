36.3% Prevention Ratio — What This Means
828 of 2,290 scenarios actively block fraud

A 36.3% prevention ratio means more than 1 in 3 fraud scenarios deployed across the ecosystem are set to actively block suspicious transactions before they complete — not just flag them for review. This is a significant milestone. Two editions ago (November 2025), prevention scenarios were 676. The sustained growth to 828 shows strategic focus on real-time blocking capabilities across the ecosystem.

These savings represent real money that fraudsters could not steal from customers. The ₹15.8 Crore figure is expected to grow significantly as more banks begin reporting fraud data (currently only 16 of 25 banks report). When all 25 banks are reporting, the actual ecosystem savings could be 2-3x higher.

Middle East — Path to 25%
From 9.8% to 25% prevention ratio

The Middle East currently has 6 prevention scenarios out of 61 total. To reach 25%, the region needs approximately 15 prevention scenarios — adding ~9 more. Key opportunity: Mashreq UAE has 14 monitoring scenarios and ₹10.4Cr in savings. Converting even 5 of these to prevention scenarios would dramatically improve the regional ratio and savings. DIB added 3 scenarios in January (+16.7%) showing appetite for growth.

India — Path to 45%
From 36.5% to 45% prevention ratio

India needs approximately 185 additional prevention scenarios to cross 45%. Key levers: AXIS (169 scenarios, 0% prevention) and SIB (49 scenarios, 0% prevention) are monitoring-only banks. Converting 20-30% of their monitoring scenarios to prevention would significantly move the needle for the entire India region.

CIMB EFM — Southeast Asia Star
77.8% prevention ratio · 34 frauds detected

CIMB EFM in the Philippines represents the gold standard for the Clari5 ecosystem. With 28 prevention scenarios out of 36 total, it achieves 77.8% — meaning nearly 4 in 5 of its fraud rules actively block rather than monitor. 34 frauds detected in January from a relatively small scenario footprint demonstrates high precision deployment. This bank's configuration should be studied as a template for other regional deployments.

Fraud Detection Growth — 79% in 3 Months
869 → 1,155 → 1,557 confirmed frauds

The 79% growth from 869 (November) to 1,557 (January) is driven by three factors: (1) More banks sharing fraud data — 7 banks in November vs 16 in January. (2) Better scenario tuning across existing deployments. (3) New scenarios added specifically targeting high-fraud patterns. This growth is expected to continue as all 25 banks begin reporting fraud outcomes.

Middle East Opportunity Analysis
Biggest growth runway in the ecosystem

The Middle East's 9.8% prevention ratio with 4 banks (DIB, RUYA, Mashreq UAE, NBQ) represents the single largest growth opportunity. Interestingly, Mashreq UAE — which has 0% prevention ratio — already saves ₹10.4 Crore monthly via monitoring. Activating prevention mode on even a subset of their 14 monitoring scenarios could multiply this impact significantly.
