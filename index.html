I4C Complaints Processed · January 2026
PNB, Canara and IDBI have deployed Clari5 CCCP products for real-time integration of I4C complaints. When a victim reports fraud on NCCRP, I4C traces funds across accounts and issues real-time freeze requests. Layer 1 = first receiving account; Layer 2 = next transfer point. Early intervention significantly improves recovery probability.
