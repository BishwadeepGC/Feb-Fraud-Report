📡 CBS / Branch — 995 Scenarios
45% of all deployed scenarios · Largest channel

CBS (Core Banking System) and Branch channels cover the backbone of banking operations — NEFT/RTGS transfers, account operations, treasury transactions, and in-branch activities. This channel has the highest scenario count because it handles the largest variety of transaction types. Fraud in CBS channels often involves large-value transfers, suspicious credits, and account manipulation patterns. Deep CBS monitoring is critical for detecting insider threats and structured fraud.

📱 Internet / Mobile Banking — 491 Scenarios
22% of all scenarios · Real-time digital prevention

Internet and Mobile Banking is the fastest-growing attack surface. Fraudsters exploit account takeover, device spoofing, beneficiary manipulation, and transaction velocity patterns. Mobile banking scenarios include multi-device login detection, unusual velocity controls, and behavioral biometrics triggers. This channel is where prevention scenarios have the highest ROI — blocking a transaction in-app before it completes is far more effective than post-execution investigation.

💸 UPI — 161 Scenarios
7% of scenarios · India-specific high-risk channel

UPI is India's dominant payment rail processing billions of transactions monthly. UPI fraud includes screen-share scams, QR code manipulation, collect request fraud, and velocity attacks. The relatively lower scenario count (7%) doesn't reflect risk — UPI transactions per bank can exceed 500M/month. Banks like PNB and Canara have dedicated UPI velocity controls. This channel is expected to grow significantly in future editions.

💳 Cards — 339 Scenarios
15% of scenarios · Debit, Credit, ATM, mPOS, eCommerce

Card fraud scenarios cover the full lifecycle — from ATM skimming detection to eCommerce suspicious merchant patterns to international card usage anomalies. The top performing scenario in the ecosystem (DC_SUSP_MERCHANT_ECOM_POS at Canara) is a card channel scenario detecting 169 frauds from 10,645 alerts. Card channels offer high prevention opportunity as transactions can be declined in real-time.

🔄 Cross Channel / General — 207 Scenarios
9% of scenarios · Behavioural & multi-channel patterns

Cross-channel scenarios detect fraud patterns that span multiple channels within a single attack flow — for example, a fraudster logging into mobile banking, changing contact details, then immediately initiating a large NEFT transfer. These scenarios are among the most sophisticated, requiring correlation of events across channels. Mule account detection (like MASHREQ UAE's top scenario) also falls into this category.
