Banks with Prevention

Prevention scenarios actively block suspicious transactions before they complete, as opposed to monitoring scenarios which flag after execution. KOTAK leads with 81.8% (305 of 373 scenarios are prevention), followed by CIMB EFM at 78%. Southeast Asia as a region achieves 78% prevention ratio — the highest globally.

Fraud detection has grown significantly — from 869 in November to 1,155 in December to 1,557 in January. This 79% increase over 3 months reflects deeper scenario coverage, improved tuning, and more banks sharing fraud data (16 of 25 banks in January vs 7 in November).

The CCCP (Cyber Crime Complaints Portal) module enables real-time integration with I4C's national fraud database. When a victim files a complaint on NCCRP, I4C traces the stolen funds and issues freeze requests layer by layer — Layer 1 (first receiving account), Layer 2 (next transfer point). Early-layer intervention significantly improves fund recovery probability.
